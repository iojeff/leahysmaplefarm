About Us

Leahy's Maple Farm began  operation in the spring of 2013 on 30 acres in Fairfax Vermont. It is a  family operation of Jane, Ed and their adult children Kevin, Jason and  Elizabeth.  We are fortunate to have many friends who have contributed to the building and development of the sugaring operation.

The sugarhouse was erected in 2012 and a forest management plan was developed to enhance the future growth of the sugarbush. In 2012

a  1350  tap vacuum base pipe line was installed.  The sugaring  operation is supported with CDL  based equipment featuring a 2.5' x 8'  CDL Intensifier Evaporator.  A CDL RO-600 system was installed in 2013  and addition tanks and taps were installed in 2014 to grow to 1600 taps.

The  sugaring operation was certified by inspectors from the Vermont Agency  of Agriculture. Leahy's Maple Farm is register with the FDA and is a  member of the Vermont Maple Sugar Makers' Association
